# RTLX 15.5.2 — Failure Evidence Capture Hardening

RTLX 15.5.2 fixes the seven verified Wolf Debugger findings from the v15.5.0 audit. The release focuses on the correctness, provenance, privacy, and boundedness of user-initiated Failure Evidence reports.

## Fixed

- `WOLF-001`: selected-element evidence is now bound to exact document identity and stale selections are discarded.
- `WOLF-002`: runtime and fixture evidence are captured under one transaction.
- `WOLF-003`: tab delivery states preserve discarded/loading/frozen/timeout/missing/invalid cases.
- `WOLF-004`: report sections distinguish unavailable, invalid, timeout, size-limited, absent, and present states.
- `WOLF-005`: sensitive selectors are redacted in Failure Evidence reports.
- `WOLF-006`: reports include capture provenance and canonical SHA-256 integrity metadata.
- `WOLF-007`: reports enforce deterministic per-section and whole-report size limits.
